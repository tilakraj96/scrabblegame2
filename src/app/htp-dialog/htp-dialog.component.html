How To Play

1.Its a two Player Game.
2.Next player should start from the alphabet present in the word mentioned by the opponent.
3.The player may write the word horizontally or vertically.
4.The word must be meaningful.
5.The points will be determined based on the length of the word and the number of vowels used.
6.Some hints may be given.
7.No repetition of words are allowed.
8.The starting word should be entered in the centre of the board.
9.The one with the highest score, wins the game.
